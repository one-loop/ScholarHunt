Welcome

Finding scholarships as an international student can be difficult and stressful, especially if you rely on outside funding for your education. That is why I created ScholarHunt, a website that allows you to search through a database of hundreds of scholarships and find the perfect match for you. To get started, go to the "Search scholarships" tab and search for a scholarship by country, university, field or name. You can then click on a relevant result to view details about the scholarships such as deadlines, eligibility and the link to the official scholarship website to apply.

We hope we have made this process easier for you to find the aid you require. As this is an open source project, we would love participants that can request to add more scholarships to the site. Currently there are 465 scholarships to search from, but more are added daily. Contributers can also write articles covering tips and additional financial information that students may find useful. Any tiny bit helps :)
